# 🔍 Phase 2: Feasibility Study & Analysis

> The feasibility study phase determines whether the project is **viable from technical, economic, legal, and operational perspectives** before committing resources to development.

## Key Stakeholders and Roles

<Tabs>
<TabItem value="pm" label="👨‍💼 Project Manager" default>

### Project Manager (PM)
- **Primary Role:** Makes the final decision to accept or reject the project
- **Responsibilities:**
  - Coordinate feasibility assessment
  - Evaluate overall project viability
  - Make go/no-go decisions
  - Communicate findings to stakeholders

</TabItem>
<TabItem value="architect" label="🏗️ Architect">

### Architect
- **Role:** Technical feasibility assessment
- **Responsibilities:**
  - Evaluate technology stack options
  - Assess technical complexity
  - Identify potential technical risks
  - Recommend architectural approaches

</TabItem>
<TabItem value="finance" label="💰 Finance Team">

### Finance Team
- **Role:** Economic feasibility assessment
- **Responsibilities:**
  - Estimate project budget
  - Calculate ROI (Return on Investment)
  - Assess cost-benefit analysis
  - Determine financial viability

</TabItem>
<TabItem value="hr" label="👥 HR Team">

### HR (Human Resources)
- **Role:** Resource availability assessment
- **Responsibilities:**
  - Check availability of required skills
  - Assess team capacity
  - Identify training needs
  - Plan resource allocation

</TabItem>
<TabItem value="ba" label="📋 Business Analyst">

### Business Analyst (BA)
- **Role:** Business feasibility assessment
- **Responsibilities:**
  - Validate business requirements
  - Assess market demand
  - Evaluate competitive landscape
  - Analyze business risks

</TabItem>
</Tabs>

## Types of Feasibility Analysis

<Tabs>
<TabItem value="technical" label="🛠️ Technical" default>

### 1. Technical Feasibility
:::success Question
**Can we build this with available technology and expertise?**
:::

**Assessment Areas:**

<details>
<summary>💻 **Technology Stack**</summary>

- Availability of required technologies
- Team expertise in chosen technologies
- Learning curve for new technologies
- Integration complexity

</details>

<details>
<summary>🏗️ **Infrastructure Requirements**</summary>

- Hardware and software needs
- Scalability requirements
- Security considerations
- Performance expectations

</details>

<details>
<summary>⚙️ **Development Complexity**</summary>

- Algorithm complexity
- Data migration challenges
- Third-party integrations
- Platform compatibility

</details>

</TabItem>
<TabItem value="economic" label="💰 Economic">

### 2. Economic Feasibility
:::success Question
**Is this project financially viable?**
:::

**Assessment Areas:**

<details>
<summary>💸 **Cost Analysis**</summary>

- Development costs (personnel, tools, infrastructure)
- Operational costs (maintenance, support)
- Opportunity costs
- Risk mitigation costs

</details>

<details>
<summary>📈 **Benefit Analysis**</summary>

- Revenue generation potential
- Cost savings
- Productivity improvements
- Strategic advantages

</details>

<details>
<summary>📊 **Financial Metrics**</summary>

- ROI (Return on Investment)
- NPV (Net Present Value)
- Payback period
- Break-even analysis

</details>

</TabItem>
<TabItem value="legal" label="⚖️ Legal">

### 3. Legal Feasibility
:::success Question
**Are there any legal constraints or compliance requirements?**
:::

**Assessment Areas:**

<details>
<summary>📋 **Regulatory Compliance**</summary>

- Industry-specific regulations
- Data protection laws (GDPR, CCPA)
- Accessibility standards
- Security standards

</details>

<details>
<summary>🔐 **Intellectual Property**</summary>

- Patent considerations
- Copyright issues
- Licensing requirements
- Third-party legal restrictions

</details>

</TabItem>
<TabItem value="operational" label="🏢 Operational">

### 4. Operational Feasibility
:::success Question
**Can the organization support and adopt this solution?**
:::

**Assessment Areas:**

<details>
<summary>👥 **Resource Availability**</summary>

- Skilled personnel
- Infrastructure capacity
- Time constraints
- Budget allocation

</details>

<details>
<summary>🔄 **Organizational Readiness**</summary>

- Change management capability
- User adoption potential
- Training requirements
- Support structure

</details>

</TabItem>
<TabItem value="schedule" label="📅 Schedule">

### 5. Schedule Feasibility
:::success Question
**Can we deliver within the required timeframe?**
:::

**Assessment Areas:**

<details>
<summary>⏰ **Timeline Analysis**</summary>

- Development duration estimates
- Resource availability windows
- Dependency management
- Critical path analysis

</details>

</TabItem>
</Tabs>

## Best Practices

:::caution Remember
A thorough feasibility study prevents costly mistakes and ensures project success.
:::

<div style={{display: 'grid', gridTemplateColumns: '1fr 1fr', gap: '2rem', margin: '2rem 0'}}>

<div>
<h3 style={{color: 'var(--ifm-color-success)', display: 'flex', alignItems: 'center', gap: '0.5rem'}}>
  ✅ Do's
</h3>

- **Involve all relevant stakeholders** in the assessment process
- **Use historical data** for more accurate estimates
- **Consider multiple scenarios** (best case, worst case, most likely)
- **Document all assumptions** and decision rationale
- **Validate findings with experts** and subject matter specialists
- **Conduct risk analysis** for each feasibility dimension
- **Create contingency plans** for identified risks

</div>

<div>
<h3 style={{color: 'var(--ifm-color-danger)', display: 'flex', alignItems: 'center', gap: '0.5rem'}}>
  ❌ Don'ts
</h3>

- **Don't rush** the feasibility assessment process
- **Don't ignore potential risks** or assume best-case scenarios
- **Don't make decisions** based on incomplete data
- **Don't overlook operational constraints** and organizational factors
- **Don't skip stakeholder validation** of findings
- **Don't forget** to consider long-term implications
- **Don't proceed** without clear go/no-go criteria

</div>

</div>
